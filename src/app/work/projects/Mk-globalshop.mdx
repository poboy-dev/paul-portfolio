## Overview

In this project,i created an onlineshop to sell medicall product.

## Key Features

- **whatsapp automated message for comande**: i intergrated a script that permit that when i want to commands i am redirected to the whatsapp of the seller and i can discuss directly with him.
- **reciep generation**: Integrated an automated process that generate and download automatically the reciep of the client.
- **Promotion management**: Established an automated system to laucnhe promotions on the website.
- **Scalable Design System**: Leveraged a design system that remains the single source of truth for both designers and developers, making it easy to maintain consistency even as the product evolves.

## Technologies Used

- **Html, Boostrap and javascript**: For building the front-end codebase with clean, reusable components.
- **Php and MySQL**: For managing database.
